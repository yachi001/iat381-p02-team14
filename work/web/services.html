In Store Services

Sell Used Books
 
Have some books you have already read lying around and want to make some quick cash?
We pay cash for your unwanted books. Bring them to the store and we will give you a quote.

*Note: An offering price will be made only when materials are presented for sale in person. Prices are based upon condition, current bookstore stock, and demand.

Donate Books
 
Have some books you have already read lying around and want to give to a good cause?
Bring your unwanted books to the store and we will donate them to BooksForLiteracy, a charitable organization in delivering literacy.

*Note: Books that are in poor condition (i.e. ripped covers, missing pages, water damaged) will not be accepted.
